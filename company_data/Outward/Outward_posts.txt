🎬✅Outward Bound Vietnam Has Just Completed The Outward Bound International Risk Management Review 2025!

We’re proud to share that Outward Bound Vietnam (OBV) has just completed the biennial Outward Bound International (OBI) Risk Management Review last week. This Review plays a key role in OBI's endorsement of Outward Bound schools like ours!

It's all about making sure we’ve got the right people, equipment, systems, and facilities to deliver safe, meaningful, and unforgettable experiences for our participants.

We extend our sincere thanks to Mr. Iain Peter – Executive Director, Outward Bound International and Ms. Malindi MacLean – Chief Executive Officer, Outward Bound New Zealand for their valuable insights and constructive feedback during last week’s review.

🎥Check out our recap video of the Review to see how we bring safety and quality to life in every program.
